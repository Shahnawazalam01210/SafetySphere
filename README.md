SafetySphere 🛡️
AI-Based Intelligent Fall and Suicide Prevention System
📌 Overview

SafetySphere is an intelligent safety and life-protection system designed to prevent accidental falls and suicide attempts from high places such as balconies, terraces, windows, and rooftops.

The system integrates AI cameras, sensors, pressure plates, safety nets, and hydraulic barriers to detect risky situations and automatically activate protective mechanisms. It also sends emergency alerts and real-time location data to rescue teams and family members.

SafetySphere focuses not only on physical safety but also on emotional support and real-time emergency response, making buildings significantly safer.

🚨 Problem Statement

Accidental falls and suicide attempts from high-rise buildings are a serious safety concern. Traditional safety measures like CCTV cameras, railings, or security guards cannot effectively detect emotional distress or sudden risky behavior.

Existing systems:

Detect incidents after they occur
Cannot analyze emotional distress
Do not provide real-time emergency alerts
Lack automated fall prevention systems

SafetySphere aims to solve these problems by combining AI monitoring, IoT sensors, and automated safety barriers.

🎯 Objectives
Prevent accidental falls and suicide attempts in buildings.
Monitor risky movements near terraces, balconies, and windows.

Automatically notify family members and emergency services.
Activate protective mechanisms like hydraulic barriers and safety nets.
Ensure secure data protection and communication.
Provide solar backup power for uninterrupted monitoring.
⚙️ System Working
Safety nets and sensors are installed around balconies, terraces, and windows.
PIR motion sensors and pressure plates detect when someone approaches a dangerous edge.
AI cameras analyze behavior and detect unusual movements or emotional distress.
If risk is detected:
An alarm is triggered
Nearby doors and windows are automatically locked
AI monitoring is activated
A hydraulic glass barrier rises automatically to stop the person.
A foldable safety net can deploy to safely catch a falling person.
Emergency alerts with live location and video feed are sent to rescue teams and family members.
